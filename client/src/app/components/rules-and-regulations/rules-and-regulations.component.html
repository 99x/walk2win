Objectives

  • Motivate employees to maintain their health prior to the Annual Medical checkup
  • Motivate employees to focus more on their health
  • Motivate employees who are finding it difficult to maintain a healthy lifestyle


Rules & Regulation

The Rules & Regulations for Walk To Win 2019 is as follows:

  • The competition will be held for three weeks from the announced date (22 July)
  • The competition will be held in two categories “Team” & “Individual”
  • Each team category requllsires four (4) Members
  • It is mandatory to have one(1) female member in each team
  • Contestants can participate in both Team & Individual category (If you register as a team, you will be automatically added to individual category)
  • Team members cannot be changed or added after the competition starts
  • Individual step count should be recorded using a mobile app or device and should sync the data to GoogleFit or added manually.
  • Leaderboard will be available in bonfire application and given URL (http://bit.ly/walkTowin)
  • Your data should sync within 5 days to our system using GoogleFit, Maximum of 7 days data will sync when you connect to app so make sure to sync your data everyday to avoid losing points.
  • The teams that display the highest scores at the end of the competition will be chosen as Winners
  • The Winners will be required to provide screenshots of the application used to record the steps count (Screenshot should contain Steps count, Date)
  • Steps counts will be recorded individually per member and cannot be merged to get points
  • Weight of all participants will be measured before the competition - Optional
  • Points calculation will be subjected to change in the purpose of motivation
  • Contestants found cheating will be disqualified from the competition as the sole purpose of this competition is to promote a healthier lifestyle
