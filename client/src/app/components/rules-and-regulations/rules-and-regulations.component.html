Rules & Regulation

The Rules & Regulations for Walk To Win 2019 is as follows:

  • The competition will be held for three weeks from the announced date (18 July)
  • The competition will be held in two categories Team & Individual
  • Each team requires four (4) Members
  • It is mandatory to have one(1) female member in each team
  • Participants can participate in both Team & Individual category (If you register as a team you will be automatically added to individual categories too)
  • Points calculation is different for Individual and Team categories - Refer the points chart
  • Team members cannot be changed or added after the competition starts
  • Individual step count should be recorded using a mobile app or device and should sync the data to GoogleFIt and leaderboard will be available in bonfire application and given URL (...)
  • The teams that display the highest scores at the end of the competition will be chosen as Winners
  • The Winners will be required to provide the screenshots of the application used to record the steps count (Screenshot should contain Steps count, Date)
  • Steps counts will be recorded individually per member and cannot be merged to get points
  • Weight of all participants will be measured before the competition
  • Points calculation will be subjective to change in the purpose of motivation
